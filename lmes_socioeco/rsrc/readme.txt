Transboundary Water Assessment Program (GEF funded project)
Marine component (Open Ocean and Large Marine Ecosystems): http://onesharedocean.org
TWAP: www.gettwap.org

Product: LMEs socio-economics database

Package content:
+ readme.txt: this file
+ version.txt: current version and history of changes
+ dataset: lmes_socioeconomics_database.xlsx, an Excel XLSX file, with multiple tabs

datasets:
An empty cell corresponds to "no data"
Inc. data means "incomplete data"; the corresponding indicator could not be computed.
+ tab "Area Population":
  - LME no: LME identification number (1 to 66)
  - LME: LME name
  - Area_Rural km2_100km: area (in km2) within 100km to the coastline, classified as Rural
  - Area_Urban km2_100km: area (in km2) within 100km to the coastline, classified as Urban
  - Area_Total km2_100km: Area_Rural + Area_Urban
  - Pop2010_Rural_100km: Population living in the rural area within 100km from the coastline
  - Pop2010_Urban_100km: Population living in the urban area within 100km from the coastline
  - Pop2010_Total_100km: Pop2010_Rural_100km + Pop2010_Urban_100km
  - 2010 Pop at 10Mx10KM: Population living in altitude lower or equal to 10m, within 100km to the coastline
  - Pop2100_Rural_100km: projection to 2100 of the rural living within 100km to the coastline
  - Pop2100_Urban_100km: projection to 2100 of the urban population within 100km to the coastline
  - Pop2100_Total_100km: Pop2100_Rural_100km + Pop2100_Total_100km
  - 2100 10M X 10KM POP SSP1: projection to 2100 (using SSP1) of the population living in altitude lower or equal to 10m within 10km to the coastline
  - 2100 10M X 10KM POP SSP3: projection to 2100 (using SSP3) of the population living in altitude lower or equal to 10m within 10km to the coastline

+ tab "Poor_NLDI_HDI":
  - LME no: LME identification number (1 to 66)
  - LME: LME name
  - NAT POVERTY LINE TOTAL COASTAL POOR (2008-2013): Number of coastal poor living below national poverty income level data available for the period 2008-2013
  - NLDI 2006_LME: Night Light Development Index, for 2006
  - HDI 2009-13: Human Development Index, for the period 2009-2013
  - SSP1 LME HDI 2100: projection (using SSP1) of HDI to 2100
  - SSP3 LME HDI 2100: projeciton (using SSP3) of HDI to 2100

+ tab "Threat Indices":
  - LME no: LME identification number (1 to 66)
  - LME: LME name
  - LME Climate Threat Index 2010
  - Maximum sea level rise 2100, m RCP 85
  - 2100 SEA LEVEL RISE THREAT INDEX SSP1
  - 2100 SEA LEVEL RISE THREAT  INDEX SSP3
  - Contemporary Threat Index

+ tab "Fish Tourism": 
Note: data correspond to the area within 100km to the coastline
  - LME no: LME identification number (1 to 66)
  - LME: LME name
  - SHELF AREA (km2)
  - Ave Annual Catch 2001-10(t)
  - Ave Annual LV (2001-10) 2013 US$
  - Ave Annual Landed Value (2001-10) 2013 US$ Millions
  - Average % LME fish contribution to animal protein of LME coastal countries 
  - Proportion of collapsed and overexploited from stock status plots (10-yr ave)
  - Ave tourism revenues (2004-2013) 2013 US$
  - Ave tourism revenues (2004-2013) 2013 US$ Millions
  - Ave % LME Tourism Contribution to GDPs of LME Coastal Countries

+ tab "Pacific Warm Pool":
  - Pacific Warm Pool Island State
  - 2010 Population
  - 2013 HDI
  - Present day Climate Threat Index
  - Sea level rise RCP 8.5 2100 (m)
  - SSP1 2100 Population
  - 2100 SSP1 HDI
  - SSP1 Sea level rise Threat Index
  - SSP3 2100 Population
  - 2100 SSP3 HDI
  - SSP3 Sea Level rise Threat Index


+ tab "Data sources": list data sources used for processing the indices
